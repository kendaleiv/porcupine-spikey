# Porcupine Spikey — Cave Adventure 🦔

A browser-based platforming side-scroller where you choose a character and navigate through underground caves!

Our oldest kid helped provide content ideas. 🙂

**[▶️ Play Now](https://kendaleiv.github.io/porcupine-spikey/)** — no download required!

## Playable Characters

| Character | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| 🦔 **Porcupine** | The original hero — brown body with silver spikes on its back |
| 🐷 **Pig** | A pink pig with pointed ears, a curly tail, and hooves |
| 🐹 **Hamster** | A golden hamster with round ears, puffy cheek pouches, and tiny paws |

## How to Play

1. Open the [hosted version](https://kendaleiv.github.io/porcupine-spikey/) or `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Choose your character and select a level from the level select screen
3. Your character automatically walks forward through the level
4. Press **SPACEBAR** or **TAP** the screen to jump
5. Jump **on top** of enemies to defeat them!
6. You can also jump **over** enemies to avoid them
7. Reach the flag at the end of the level to win!

## Enemies

### Level 1 — Cave Adventure

| Enemy | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 🫎 **Moose** | Stationary — jump over or land on them |
| 🐧 **Penguin** | Slides toward you — time your jump carefully |
| 🦕 **Dinosaur** | Large and stationary — jump over or stomp |
| 🧙 **Spikey Ball Thrower** | Throws arcing spikey balls at you — dodge or stomp |

### Level 2 — Ice Cave

All Level 1 enemies plus:

| Enemy | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| ⛄ **Snowman** | Throws snowballs at you — dodge or stomp |
| 🦈 **Stingray** | Glides toward you with a wave motion — time your jump carefully |
| 🦔 **Spiky Guy** | Throws pretzels that land on the ground — dodge or stomp |

⚠️ Watch out for **falling icicles** — they drop from the ceiling when you get close!

## Running Locally

Just open the file directly in your browser:

```
open index.html
```

Or serve it with any static file server:

```
npx serve .
```
